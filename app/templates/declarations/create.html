Passive Transport

Required only when movement type is 3a (RoRo Accompanied). Describes the trailer type.

Carrier EORI

Must start with XI or EU country prefix.

GB EORIs not accepted for carriers on ENS.

For Maritime and RoRo movement types, TSS expects carrier name, street/number, city, postcode, and country before header submission.
