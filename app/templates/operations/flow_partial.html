Declaration Types At A Glance
Based on the TSS cheat sheet prefixes and journey order.
ENS
Entry Summary Declaration
ENS000... / DEC000...
Safety and security declaration submitted before goods move into NI.
When: GB to NI RoRo and air inventory-linked flows.
SFD
Simplified Frontier Declaration
SFD000... / DEC000...
Auto-generated by TSS when the ENS consignment is accepted and progressed into the simplified route.
When: Created automatically after TSS progresses the consignment.
SDI
Supplementary Declaration
SUP000...
Full customs completion after arrival. Trader updates the TSS draft and submits.
When: Deadline is the 10th day of the following month.
GMR
Goods Movement Reference
GMR000... / GVMS
Links the physical vehicle to the authorised customs movement and produces the barcode for the driver.
When: Created after consignments are authorised for movement.
Step By Step TSS Simplified Procedure
Official Route A sequence from the cheat sheet, adapted to this workspace.
1
Create Declaration Header
POST declaration_headers (create)
Creates the movement level ENS reference with transport, route and arrival details.
2
Create Consignment And Goods
POST consignments / POST goods
Build the declaration hierarchy: DEC consignment first, then up to 99 goods items below it.
3
Submit Consignment In TSS
POST consignments (submit)
The DEC consignment is submitted after goods exist, then TSS processes it and auto-generates the SFD or EIDR path for the simplified procedure.
4
Create And Submit GMR
POST gvms_gmr (create / submit)
Only after consignments are authorised for movement. This is the vehicle movement control step.
5
Lookup, Update And Submit SDI
GET supplementary_declarations / POST supplementary_declarations
Lookup by SFD number, enrich valuation and customs fields, then submit before the monthly deadline.

GB to NI RoRo standard movement. ENS to SFD or EIDR to GMR, then SDI close-out only where the simplified flow requires it.
