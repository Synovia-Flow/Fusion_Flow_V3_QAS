ENS Consignment Management

The Consignment resource (/consignments) is the core unit of the ENS process. Each consignment belongs to a Declaration Header (ENS000...) and represents one importer/exporter pairing. On submit, TSS creates the ENS to HMRC and auto-generates an SFD (or EIDR for non-controlled goods).

Goods Item Management

The Goods resource (/goods) manages individual goods lines. Max 99 per consignment. Critical: goods_id differs across ENS, SFD, and SDI — always use Lookup to get the correct ID for each stage.

LookupGET /goods?sfd_number= or ?sup_dec_number= or ?ens_number=Valid parent ref.List: goods_id, description, marks
ReadGET /goods?reference=hex...&fields=...Valid goods_id.All requested fields + nested

Supplementary Declaration Management

The SDI (/supplementary_declarations) is the post-arrival full customs declaration. TSS auto-generates a draft from SFD data. Trader updates with valuation, procedures, documents. Deadline: 10th calendar day of month following arrival.

3.1 Operations

OperationMethodEndpointPre-conditionsReturns
Lookup (from SFD)GET/supplementary_declarations?sfd_number=DEC...Valid SFD ref.SUP000... ref
ReadGET/supplementary_declarations?reference=SUP...&fields=...Valid ref.Fields + nested
FilterGET/supplementary_declarations?filter=status=...Valid status.List of SUP refs
UpdatePOST/supplementary_declarations {update}Draft or TIR.Updated
SubmitPOST/supplementary_declarations {submit}All mandatory. Goods complete.status: "submitted"
RecallPOST/supplementary_declarations {recall}Pending Payment.Returns to Draft
Reclassify H1→H8POST/supplementary_declarations {reclassify}UKIMS holder.New GLR000... ref
Standalone CreatePOST/supplementary_declarations {create}EIDR authorised.SUP000... ref

3.2 SDI Statuses

StatusDescriptionAction
DraftAuto-created from SFD. Awaiting trader.Update + submit before 10th deadline.
SubmittedSent to CDS.Duties being calculated. Poll.
AcceptedCDS accepted. MRN allocated.Read duty. Payment required.
RejectedCDS rejected.Read error_message. Fix + re-submit.
Trader Input RequiredTSS needs action.Check error_message.
Pending PaymentDuties calculated, awaiting payment.Pay or Recall to fix.
ClearedPaid. Goods released.Terminal success.
Amendment RequiredCDS needs corrections.Update + re-submit.
CancelledSDI cancelled.Terminal.
